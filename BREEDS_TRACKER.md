## Notes

- Rescue breeds are generic mixes, not purebreds
- Common tier: Beginner-friendly, affordable
- Uncommon tier: Moderate stats, moderate price
- Rare tier: High stats, expensive or gem-only
- Exotic tier: Very high stats, mostly gems
- Legendary tier: Best stats, gems only, very limited
